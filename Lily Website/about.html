Our Story

LILLYBEZ Limited was founded from a simple but powerful vision—to make the process of finding and providing quality accommodation more straightforward, accessible, and reliable.

The journey began in 2022, built through hands-on experience working closely with landlords and gaining a practical understanding of the rental market. What started as a small, passion-driven effort gradually developed into a structured approach to connecting people with suitable homes.

In 2026, LILLYBEZ Limited was officially registered, marking a key milestone in its growth as a recognised property solutions business.

Who We Are Today

Today, LILLYBEZ Limited operates as a property solutions company focused on connecting landlords with suitable tenants and supporting individuals in finding quality accommodation.

We specialise in a rent-to-rent style approach, where we help secure and manage tenant occupancy while ensuring landlords receive agreed rental income.

Landlords retain responsibility for property maintenance and repairs, while we focus on tenant placement and smooth letting coordination.
